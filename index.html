iGuess

Use the keyboard or click a letter

Mechanics

  • A secret word will be randomly generated for you to guess.
  • Start guessing by using the keyboard to type the letters. You may use the mouse to click or tap the letter if you are using a mobile phone or tablet.
  • You have a maximum of 5wrong guesses.
  • If your guess matches any of the letter in the secret word, it will be revealed in the boxes.
  • If you reached the maximum number of wrong guesses, game will be over.
